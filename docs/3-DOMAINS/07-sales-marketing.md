# Sales & Marketing Domain

> **Status:** Draft  

## Models

- `Campaign` — Marketing campaigns
- `Referral` — Referral program
- `Coupon` — Discount codes

## Owns

- Marketing campaigns
- Referral program and tracking
- Discount/coupon management
- Landing pages and CMS pages
- SEO and marketing content

## Depends On

- **Products** — Campaigns and coupons reference products
- **Orders** — Coupons apply to orders

## Does NOT Own

- Product catalog or pricing
- Managed Stock or Physical Inventory
- Order lifecycle
- Payment processing
